About Red Bull

Our Story

Red Bull Energy Drink was launched in 1987 and created the energy drink category worldwide. Through exclusive and innovative marketing concepts, Red Bull has established itself as the leading energy drink in the market.

The unique combination of high-quality ingredients – caffeine, taurine, B-group vitamins, and sucrose and glucose – developed the product into a favorite among athletes, students, and professionals in highly demanding situations.

Our Mission

Red Bull gives wings to people and ideas. The company's mission is to support and promote these activities all over the world through its "Gives You Wings" philosophy.

We are committed to the highest level of quality and continuously work to improve our products and develop new exciting variants to meet the evolving needs of our consumers.
